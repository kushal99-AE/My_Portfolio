How it All Begin - My Journey into Artificial Intelligence & Autonomy

My fascination with engineering began in childhood, captivated by the way machines moved and operated. What started as an obsession with spinning wheels soon evolved into a deep curiosity about automobiles, aircraft, and—ultimately—the ability of industrial machines to think, decide, and act on their own.

During my aerospace studies, I was drawn to the complex problem of autonomy—how to make an aircraft, a vehicle, or a robot navigate and make real-time decisions without human intervention. I realized that autonomy wasn’t just about movement; it was about intelligence, perception, and control—the foundation of AI-driven robotics.

This passion led me to specialize in AI and autonomy, recognizing that intelligence was the key to unlocking true machine independence. After earning my Bachelor’s in Aerospace Engineering, I pursued a Postgraduate Program in AI & ML, diving deep into computer vision, deep learning, and autonomous decision-making. Now as a Masters Student in Machine Learning Engineering , I am expanding my expertise in perception, self learning, AI Algorithms, and to futher deep dive into AI to redefine autonomy acorss industries
